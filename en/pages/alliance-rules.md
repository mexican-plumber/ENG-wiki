# Alliance Rules

1. NO Bullying or Harassment! 
If you feel you are being targeted in any way, please contact any of the leadership team.

2. Treat Alliance members with respect. 
Should a disagreement arise and cannot be resolved amicably between parties involved, please contact leadership to assist.

3. Inactivity: Should you have a planned period of inactivity longer that 24 Hours please notify any of the Leadership team. 
    - Real Life will always take priority but please be considerate and communicate if something unexpected comes up as soon as reasonably possible. Maximum of 4 days then you will be kicked out of the alliance.

4. Pay attention and listen to R4/R5 messages. If asked to do something, please do it in a reasonable time frame.

5. Keep up to date with State Rules.
    - It is your responsibility to ensure you are not contravening any of the rules. Should you be found to be in contravention of state rules, punishment will be issued against you. This may be anything from verbal warning to being kicked from the alliance.

6. You are not to gather resources/farm in other alliances territory. (Unless we have an agreement) 
    - Other alliances are allowed to attack you to remove you from their territory,  you are not allowed to retaliate. If you retaliate it will be seen as contravening state rules.
